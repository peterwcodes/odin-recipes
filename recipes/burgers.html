Burgers Recipe

Description

Burgers are a delicious treat to enjoy during any ocasion. Crispy and savory, these treats will be sure to be a hit wherever you bring them.

Burgers have quickly spread across the entire United States, as food-goers continue to love them!

Ingredients

  • Raw cheese burgers
  • Cheese
  • Burger buns
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Pat burgers dry with paper towel.
  2. Season burgers with salt and pepper. Mix it all in with your hands to ensure the seasoning is evenly distributed throughout.
  3. Let burgers rest for 30 minutes.
  4. Turn stove on to medium high using a cast iron pan.
  5. Put burgers onto pan, letting them get a nice char on both sides. About 5 minutes each.
  6. In a separate pan, toast burger buns.
  7. After flipping burgers, wait about a minute, then place cheese slices on top of burgers.
  8. Remove everything, build your burgers, and enjoy!
