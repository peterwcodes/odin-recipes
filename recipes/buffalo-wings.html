Buffalo Wings Recipe

Description

Buffalo Wings are a delicious treat to enjoy during any ocasion. Spicy and savory, these treats will be sure to be a hit wherever you bring them.

Originating in Buffalo, NY in the 1970s, Buffalo Wings have quickly spread across the entire United States, as food-goers continue to love them!

Ingredients

  • Chicken wings
  • Buffalo sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Pat chicken wings dry with paper towel.
  2. Season wings with salt and pepper. Mix it all in with your hands to ensure the seasoning is evenly distributed throughout.
  3. Preheat the air fryer to 400 degrees farenheit. Let chicken wings rest for 30 minutes.
  4. Put chicken wings in the air fryer for 30 minutes, flipping every 7-10 minutes.
  5. Remove chicken wings from air fryer and put into large mixing bowl. Pour buffalo sauce over wings and toss to coat wings.
  6. Enjoy with some cellery or simply on its own!
