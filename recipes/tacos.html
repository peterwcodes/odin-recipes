Tacos Recipe

Description

Tacos are a delicious treat to enjoy during any ocasion. Spicy and savory, these treats will be sure to be a hit wherever you bring them.

Tacos have quickly spread across the entire United States, as food-goers continue to love them!

Ingredients

  • 1.25 pounds of Chicken thighs
  • Small, corn tortillas
  • Salsa
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Cajun seasoning
  • Paprika

Steps

  1. Pat chicken thighs dry with paper towel.
  2. Cut them into small bite-size pieces.
  3. Season chicken with olive oil, cajun, salt, and paprika. Mix it all in with your hands to ensure the seasoning is evenly distributed throughout.
  4. Let chicken rest for 30 minutes.
  5. Turn stove on to medium high using a pan.
  6. Put chicken onto pan, letting them get a nice char on all sides. About 10 minutes.
  7. In a separate pan, toast taco tortillas.
  8. Remove everything, build your tacos, and enjoy!
